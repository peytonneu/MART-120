A CLI seems to look more intimidating compared to GUI but is shown to have an advantage for the manipulation of files and directories quicker. GUI is more on the simpler side that we are more used to seeing. The visual elements of icons and buttons showing our directories and files making it more user friendly for us. CLI also shows text-only commands and is more traditional, while GUI uses icons and buttons. CLI is more of a learning curve to some, while GUI is meant to be easier to use and learn, be it on PC or MAC.
